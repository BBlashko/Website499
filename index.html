The Lookout
Home Monitoring System

University of Victoria
ELEC/SENG 499 Design Project
Supervised by Dr. Xiaodai Dong
Sponsored by CanAssist

Project

The Lookout is a standalone home monitoring system that detects when there is no activity in its vicinity for unusual periods of time. We are partnered with CanAssist to create a Non-invasive “Non-Activity Monitor”. Commercially available home monitoring systems exist are intrusive, costly, and require an internet connection as well monthly fees and professional installation. Many seniors and other vulnerable populations who live alone in their own home often forget to wear their medical alert devices. For an example, if someone has left the house or fallen the Lookout sends a message to a family member or caregiver since an irregular amount (or lack) of activity was detected.
